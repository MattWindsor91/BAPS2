Add a track list entry

There are two ways to add audio into your track lists.
  1. The first is to use audio from the predefined audio collections at the top of the interface:


    You can click and drag items from the Audio Collections and drop them into any of your track lists. They will always add themselves to the end of the list. You can then move them to the desired position.
    The long thin button above each Audio Collection refreshes the list and will retrieve any recently added items
  2. The second method is to add tracks from the Music Library.
    To do this click the button marked 'Search Record Library':



    This will open the search form which you can use to find your desired track:



    Type in the name of an artist and/or a track name and click search. You do not have to type the full artist or track name and you may use '%' as a wildcard if you do not know exactly how to spell a name.
    For example:
    Entering: fre%erc in the artist box and clicking search will find all the tracks by Freddy Mercury, the middle letters of his name being filled in automatically.

    Search terms are case InSeNsItIvE. You will get a maximum of 200 search results, if you receive 200 results then it is likely that there are more tracks that meet your criteria but you will have to be more specific to get them.



    Once you have found the track that you want you select it by clicking on it and then choose which list to add it to using the large buttons at the bottom of the form. Once you have got all of your audio use the close button at the top of the form to close the search page.
